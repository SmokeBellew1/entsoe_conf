The Venue

The ENTSOE-E Annual Conference 2015 will take place in the Crowne Plaza Hotel, located in the heart of Brussels near Rogier.

Hotel

The venue for this years conference is the Crone Plaza Hotel Brussels

If you would like to book a room ahead of the conference you can do so at crowneplaza.com →

Travel

Airport:
The hotel is easily accessible from both Brussels National Airport and Charleroi. A special room rate for attendees has been arranged - [Get Conference Rate](https://aws.passkey.com/event/14077958/owner/7604429/home)

Train:
The hotel is easily accessible from Brussels - Gare du Midi, Gare Central and Gare du Nord.

Public Transport (STIB):
The nearest Metro station to the hotel is Rogier - Lines 2 & 6. The nearest tram stop is Rogier - Trams 3 & 4
